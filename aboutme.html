About Me

Passionate about public service and shaping policy, I am a driven young professional with hands-on experience in political internships. Throughout my career, I’ve had the opportunity to work alongside dedicated leaders, communicate with strong teams, and gain strategic skills. I’m particularly interested in government affairs, campaign management, and advocacy. I’m committed to creating positive change through impactful work in the political sphere. With a keen understanding of the political landscape and a desire to contribute meaningfully, I look forward to growing and learning in this dynamic field.

Though my career and educational pursuits are at the forefront of my mind, I stay balanced in all aspects of my life. The arts have been apart of my life since childhood. Singing and dancing have been a constant both in and out of school. These passions keep me grounded and creative, often lending a hand in my educational thinking processes.
